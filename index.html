About Me

As a native Virginian haling from the once known "Peanut Capital of the World", I'm what some would refer to as a "Suffolk Peanut." In fact, there's a saying where I come from that sums up the small-town's ego: "I was peanut born, I was peanut-bred, and when I die I'll be peanut-dead." But alas, Richmond very much captured my heart and I have happily lived here for the last 3.5 years!

I am married to my amazing husband and together we have no children yet, but one perfect tuxedo-cat named Matisse. I love nature and being active so most of my hobbies revolve around these. Horseback riding, gardening, and hiking are my favorites!

I love animals and I have been working as a Licensed Veterinary Technician for the past 6.5 years. My husband and I are both music enthusiasts and try to patron local and touring punk, darkwave, and hardcore shows whenever our busy schedules allow it. Occasionally I slap the bass and hit the drums for fun in the confines of my own home where no one can hear it. Visual art is an interest of mine as well and I specifically enjoy drawing with pen and ink and dabbling in design.

I'm looking forward to a new career in web developement and can't wait to discover all of the opportunities it will allow me!
